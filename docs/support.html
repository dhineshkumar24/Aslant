ASLANT: Drop — Support

Thanks for playing ASLANT: Drop. This page covers quick help for the iOS app.

How to play

  • Tap anywhere to start
  • Hold and drag to move the lantern
  • Avoid the falling shards — survive as long as you can
  • Audio starts after your first tap

Your best score

Your personal best is saved on your device only. It is not synced to a server. Uninstalling the app clears it.

Privacy

See our Privacy Policy. We do not collect personal data.
