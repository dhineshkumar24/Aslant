ASLANT: Drop — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

ASLANT: Drop ("the App") is a single-player arcade game. We respect your privacy and keep data collection to a minimum.

Data we collect

We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

The App does not require an account, email address, or sign-in.

Data stored on your device

The App saves your personal best score locally on your device using on-device storage (WebView local storage). This value:

  • Stays on your phone or tablet
  • Is not sent to our servers (we have no servers for this app)
  • Is removed if you uninstall the App or clear app data

Third-party services

The App does not use analytics, advertising networks, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Children

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from anyone.

Changes

We may update this policy if the App changes. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy: use the Support URL listed on the App Store page for ASLANT: Drop.
